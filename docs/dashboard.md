# Public Dashboard Guide

The BEACON Dashboard is a public-facing digital greeter featuring **Buddy**, your AI companion.

## Features
*   **Weather & Pulse:** Real-time localized weather data, precipitation chances, and a generated 1-2 sentence "Pulse" about your city's current rhythm.
*   **Daily Agenda:** Click the **🗓️** icon to see today's verified local events. You can upvote or downvote events to influence their visibility!
*   **5-Day Outlook:** Click the **📅** icon for a high-level summary of the coming week's weather pattern.
*   **Interaction:** Buddy reacts to the time of day, weather severity, and even changes his outfit based on his current "station." Click the various location pods to see what he's up to!

## Secret Menu
*Shhh!* If you tap the main header 5 times quickly (or type secrets on a keyboard), you might find a hidden menu with themes, games, and other fun interactions.
