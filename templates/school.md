# School & Dynamic Beacons Guide

School dashboards are tailored experiences derived from the primary BEACON template, designed to serve localized weather and Emergency Action Plan (EAP) information.

## Features
*   **Localized Context:** Dynamic beacons query weather exclusively for their configured zip codes and receive an AI "Pulse" observation tailored specifically to their city or school name.
*   **School Closings:** During winter months, an automated EUP closings ticker may appear across the bottom of the screen.
*   **Emergency Banners:** If your facility is subscribed to an EAP Multicast group (like "I Love U Guys" protocols), the dashboard will immediately lock and display full-screen color-coded safety instructions when an alert is broadcasted on the network.
