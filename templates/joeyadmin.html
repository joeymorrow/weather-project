👋 Welcome to CoolAdmin (Admin Onboarding)

If you are a newly assigned administrator, this dashboard is the master "cockpit" for the BEACON ecosystem. It can look intimidating, but it is grouped logically. Hover over any yellow ? icon for a quick explanation of a feature.

  • APIs & Budgets: BEACON uses external AIs. If the system stops generating text, check here to ensure you haven't hit your daily free limits.
  • Endpoints & Sites: Manage and monitor the live dashboards displayed on your TVs or at specific schools.
  • AI Content: The AI automatically scrapes and populates events. If it hallucinates or makes a mistake, you can manually delete or edit the event here to correct it.
  • AI Tuning: You can give the AI "Custom Instructions" (Prompts) or feed it specific website URLs (Vetted Sources) to control exactly where it gets its news.
